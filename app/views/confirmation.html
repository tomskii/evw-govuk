What happens next?

We will now check your details before we issue your electronic visa waiver - this can take up to 24 hours.

We will email within 24 hours with details of how to download it.

Please add electronicvisawaiver@homeoffice.gsi.gov.uk to your safe senders list to make sure you get your download details. You should wait 24 hours before contacting us about your EVW

Changing your details

Email electronicvisawaiver@homeoffice.gsi.gov.uk if you need to change your electronic visa waiver details​, eg if your UK arrival date has changed.

Any changes must be made at least 48 hours before you travel to the UK.
